>> The Reddit COVID dataset <<
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/pavellexyr/the-reddit-covid-dataset

All the mentions of COVID-19 on Reddit, up to 25th of October, 2021.
The ongoing outbreak of COVID-19 is the largest pandemic many of us will face in our entire lives. 
In interests of evaluating public opinion and leveraging social data for public health, we release 
this dataset online under a CC-BY license.
